Build-a-Bite

A service for the helpless baker



Do you have trouble just baking ready-make cookies? Do you need baked goods for a party? Look no further than Build-a-Bite. Supply a recipe or ingredients and I will bake them for you! Prices are based on the complexity of a recipe or the number of unique ingredients (ie. ingredients that are not common to most recipes; see list below.)



Common ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar (white + brown)
  • Flour
  • Baking soda + powder
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
